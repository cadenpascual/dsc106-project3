      • Decision Rationale

      Our project mainly focused on visualizing glucose level over time using data collected from patients from wearable devices. By using D3.js we created an interactive scatter plot to help explore the glucose levels and its relationship with the dietary intake of certain macronutrients. The main goal is to provide insights into daily glucose fluctuations and how they respond to meals. In terms of filtering we decided to filter the glucose data by the corresponding macronutrient given their logged time and amount. In terms of data filtering the glucose measurement data was taken in consistent 15 minute intervals giving us structured and clean data to work with. The logged food data points on the other hand was not nearly as uniform or structured, having seemingly arbitrary time inputs that would jump around.
